# Phase 15 Plan 02: Real Qwen3.5-2B GRPO Training Tuning Matrix

## Objective

Turn the formal Verl case into a real Qwen3.5-2B + geometry3k GRPO training loop, then search from single-card BS=1 toward the largest stable single-card and 8-card throughput configurations.

## Threat Model

- **T-15-02-01 validation-only regression:** The runner must not accidentally keep `trainer.val_only=True` for Phase 15 training cases.
- **T-15-02-02 fake throughput:** A case that exits before completing 3 training steps cannot be counted as a passed throughput result.
- **T-15-02-03 survivor bias:** OOM/resource failures must be retained in rows and reports so the tuning boundary is visible.
- **T-15-02-04 hardware overcommit:** Single-card tuning must constrain visible devices before escalating to 8 cards.
