# Phase 15 Plan 03: Verl Stage Timing Extraction From W&B And Raw Logs

## Objective

Extract the detailed GRPO phase timings that Verl prints or logs to W&B, then make them durable and visible in the final report.

## Threat Model

- **T-15-03-01 thin W&B summaries:** Offline W&B summary files may omit per-step timing history; raw logs must be a fallback source.
- **T-15-03-02 misleading stage names:** Parser output must preserve original metric keys alongside normalized stage names.
- **T-15-03-03 unproven instrumentation:** If code patches are needed inside Verl or dependencies, provenance must identify the exact repo, branch, commit, and pushed URL.
- **T-15-03-04 aggregate hides spikes:** Store per-step timing rows, not only averages.
